Anime Boy

I sail the seas with a straw hat crown,
With dreams so big they weigh me down.
Through storms and wars, I never flee,
The Pirate King's what I'm meant to be.

My friends, my crew, they stand by me,
Together we chase our destiny.
Each island holds a secret song,
In this wide world, we all belong.

With scarred hands and hopeful eyes,
I reach for freedom across the skies.
Gold and fame are not my goal-
I chase the sea to free my soul.
