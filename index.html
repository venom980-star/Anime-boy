Straw Hat Spirit

A hat of straw, a heart so bold,

A tale of dreams the sea has told.

With fists of fire and soul so free,

He sails to claim his destiny.

Luffy, the name the world shall shout,

Through storm and pain, he has no doubt.

With friends who stand through thick and thin,

He fights with fire that burns within.

So when you hear the ocean sing,

Remember this: the Pirate King.
